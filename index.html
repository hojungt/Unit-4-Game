Instruction:


You receive a random number between 19 and 180 every round.

Each magic crystal holds a different number between 1 to 12. Clicking on a crystal will add its number to your total score!

You win the game if your total score matches the random number, and you lose the game if it exceeds the random number.

Lose three rounds and you are OUT!
